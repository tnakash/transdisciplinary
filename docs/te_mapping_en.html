Transdisciplinary Engineering Literature Mapping

Papers whose Title, Abstract, or Keywords contain "transdisciplinary engineering" were collected from the ISTE (formerly ISPE) Transdisciplinary Engineering international conference proceedings (2016–2025, IOS Press, open access) and related journals, then evaluated and mapped on three indicators — Goal, Collaboration, and Integration — based on the framework of Lattanzio et al. (2021). 日本語版はこちら
